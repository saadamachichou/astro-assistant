## Process

ASTROQODELABS generally works through these steps:
1. Discovery: understand the client goals and context.
2. Project brief: clarify the idea, audience, features, and constraints.
3. Scope: define the service path, technical needs, timeline target, and investment range.
4. Design: shape the user experience and interface direction.
5. Development: build frontend, backend, integrations, and infrastructure as needed.
6. Testing: review functionality, performance, responsiveness, and reliability.
7. Launch: deploy and support the first release.
8. Support: discuss improvements, maintenance, or next features when useful.
